Project Scope

A site-specific sound capture & composition instrument

The Vision

Oh My Ondas is a portable instrument that captures the unique sonic signature of a specific place and time, transforming environmental sounds into musical compositions that could only exist at that exact moment and location. Every recording is a sonic photograph — inextricably linked to its geographic coordinates, temporal data, and environmental audio fingerprint.

What It Is

Oh My Ondas combines the tactile immediacy of hardware samplers like the Roland SP-404 with modern features: GPS location binding, AI-assisted composition, and internet radio sampling. It's designed for musicians, field recordists, and sound artists who want to create music that's authentically tied to place and moment.

♫ Sampling Engine

8 sample slots with 30 seconds each, variable speed playback, reverse, loop, and granular effects.

☰ Step Sequencer

8-track pattern sequencer with up to 64 steps, P-locks, euclidean rhythms, and trigger conditions.

♬ Synthesizer

Subtractive synth with multiple oscillators, filter, and ADSR envelope for melodic content.

↺ Effects Chain

24 effect types including delay, reverb, filter, granular, and punch-in performance FX.

⚑ Location Binding

GPS coordinates embedded in every recording. Local radio station discovery based on position.

★ AI Composition

Generate patterns and arrangements based on "vibes" — contextual AI that responds to location and time.

Design Philosophy

  • No Dropdowns Every control is a button. Hardware-ready interface that maps directly to physical controls without menu diving.
  • Hold-to-Activate Fill and punch FX use momentary hold patterns, designed for live performance with physical buttons.
  • Visual Feedback First LED timing, pad animations, and VU meters designed around hardware response times.
  • Place-Time Authenticity Every composition is unique because it incorporates unrepeatable elements: exact sounds, radio content, weather, and time.
  • Open & DIY Open source design enabling community builds, modifications, and custom firmware.

Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Web Prototype

Full-featured web application demonstrating all UI/UX patterns. Touch support, keyboard shortcuts, all panels functional.

Phase 2: Hardware Design

Finalize enclosure, component selection, and PCB design. Create bill of materials and assembly guide.

Phase 3: Firmware Development

Port audio engine to Teensy. Implement touch pads, encoders, and display integration.

Phase 4: Integration

WiFi/GPS connectivity, AI API integration, and full system testing.

Phase 5: Documentation

Complete build guide, user manual, and community resources for DIY builders.

Out of Scope (For Now)

The following features are intentionally deferred to keep the initial build focused:

  • Multi-track recording (DAW-style)
  • CV/Gate outputs for modular integration
  • Built-in speaker
  • Video/visual generation
  • Cloud sync and social features
  • Mobile app companion
