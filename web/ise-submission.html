1.1 Company Profile (B2Match)

Company Name
Oh My Ondas
Short Description (max 200 chars)
A sonic camera that photographs soundscapes. Captures the sounds of a place and transforms them into music that remembers where it was made. Combine AI, GPS, and real-time audio to create unrepeatable sonic pictures.
Full Description
Oh My Ondas is a sonic camera - a professional instrument that photographs the soundscape of a place and transforms it into music. Like a camera captures light, Oh My Ondas captures the sounds of a location - ambient noise, radio signals, environmental audio - and lets you create music that remembers and represents that specific place. You can listen back and recall the place, or interact with it in real time. Our technology combines: - 8-track professional sequencer with parameter locks and conditional triggers - Real-time environmental audio capture (microphone, radio, contact sensors) - GPS and timestamp embedding for location context - AI-assisted composition that responds to the character of a place - Web Audio API implementation with full browser compatibility WORKING PROTOTYPE AVAILABLE NOW: Live Demo: https://alevm.github.io/oh-my-ondas/ Source Code: https://github.com/alevm/oh-my-ondas Current version (v2.4.6) features: - 8-track sequencer with scene morphing (A/B/C/D) - 16-pad sampler with 3 sound kits - Dual-oscillator synthesizer with filter and ADSR - Internet radio integration - 8 real-time effects (delay, granular, glitch, bitcrush, stutter, reverb, filter, tape) - GPS tracking with automatic location embedding - AI pattern generation with landmark awareness - Session recording with metadata export Seeking partnerships with museums, cultural venues, broadcast media, and event organizers.

Solution 1: Museums & Cultural Venues

Target: Moco Museum, CaixaForum+
Title
Site-Specific Audio Experiences for Museums & Cultural Venues
Short Description
A sonic camera for museums - captures the soundscape of each gallery room and transforms it into music visitors can remember and interact with.
Full Description
CHALLENGE: Museums struggle to create memorable audio experiences. Traditional audio guides are generic and disconnected from the actual space. SOLUTION: Oh My Ondas photographs the soundscape of each gallery room - capturing ambient sounds, visitor presence, environmental audio - and transforms them into music that represents that specific place. CAPABILITIES: - Captures and transforms room soundscapes into music - Visitors create sonic memories they can take home - AI-generated compositions that reflect the character of a space - Real-time interaction with the sounds of a place APPLICATIONS: - Immersive gallery installations driven by real room sounds - Visitor participation in collective soundscape compositions - "Sonic postcards" - music that remembers a specific room and moment - Interactive experiences where visitors shape the music of a place SPECS: Browser-based, 8 channels, <10ms latency, GPS-aware DEMO: https://alevm.github.io/oh-my-ondas/ ADVANTAGE: Only solution that creates music FROM a place's actual sounds - not just played at a location.
